> Calling all enthusiasts who love the thrill of problem-solving and quizzing! Academic Triathlon is the ultimate battleground where participants engage in classic quizzing with an exciting twist. The three exciting phases of the triathlon will certainly pose as an intellectually stimulating challenge:
>
> - Integration Bee: Solve tricky integration questions
> - Fermi Bee: Provide well-reasoned guesses to approximation problems
> - Cryptic Hunt: A final, exciting (and surprise) challenge to close out the triathlon!
>
> Ensure that your teams stay at the top of the points tables throughout the three stages for a chance at our 10k+ INR Prize Pool!
>
